About Me

Hello! I'm Maura, owner of 3two9 image & design. I'm a native of Tacoma, Washington. As one of 6 kids, I loved documenting family functions from an early age. I currently live in Port Orchard, with my husband of 20 years and 2 busy sons.

Even though I've always had a camera in hand as long as I can remember, I started taking photography seriously 2 years ago after my best friend of 30 years passed away from pancreatic cancer. Her advice to me was "Don't wait. Whatever you want to do, try or accomplish....don't wait. Use your talent. Be happy." I think she would be proud to know I actually listened.

I currently photograph weddings, highschool seniors, sports and families and hope to start including my passion for dentistry by doing staff photos for local offices. Don't see what you are looking to have photographed? Just ask!

I also do photo/video tributes and presentations for family, businesses and events.

Need a high volume of photos scanned? I do that too!
